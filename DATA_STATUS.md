## What "Verified" Means

A species entry is **VERIFIED** when its JSON has both `verifiedDate` (ISO date) and `sourceUrl` (link to the official agency page or PDF) populated. This means every fee, deadline, and rule on that species card was cross-checked against the official source on that date.

A species entry is **ESTIMATED** otherwise — compiled from secondary sources (huntingData.ts, hunting media, prior-year brochures) and may have errors. Hunters making financial decisions should always confirm against the state agency.

## Verification Workflow

1. Pick the top entry from the **Priority Queue** above.
2. Open that state's official big-game brochure PDF.
3. Cross-check every fee, deadline, point-system rule, and OTC detail in the species's JSON entry against the brochure. Paste the relevant section into chat with Claude — it'll flag discrepancies and propose edits.
4. Update the JSON file with corrections. Add `verifiedDate` (today's ISO date) and `sourceUrl` (link to the brochure or the official big-game page).
5. Run `npm run data-status` to regenerate this file.
6. Commit: `data(regulations): verify <state> <species> against <agency>`.
